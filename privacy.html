Rias Gremory Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Rias Gremory is a Discord bot used for AI chat, moderation, support tickets, leveling, welcome/goodbye messages, and server management features.

Data We Process

The bot may process Discord user IDs, server IDs, channel IDs, message content, message attachments, moderation records, XP/level data, and ticket messages depending on which features are enabled by server administrators.

AI chat is only active in channels enabled by administrators. AI memory is temporary, user-specific, limited to recent context, and automatically cleared after about 30 minutes of inactivity.

Voice AI is restricted to administrators. It only runs when explicitly started by an authorized administrator. The bot does not store audio files or persistent voice transcripts.

Ticket transcripts may be created only inside ticket channels when transcript logging is enabled by administrators. Users are informed in the ticket channel that the ticket may be recorded for support and moderation safety. Transcript files are stored locally, include the ticket owner ID for deletion handling, and are automatically deleted after 30 days.

Leveling data stores user IDs and XP/level values. Moderation records may store user IDs, moderator IDs, action type, reason, and timestamp.

How Data Is Used

Data is used only to provide bot features such as AI responses, moderation, support tickets, leveling, role rewards, and server management. Data is not sold and is not used for advertising or user profiling.

Message content is not used to train machine learning or AI models by the bot owner. It is processed only to provide bot functionality, such as AI responses or ticket transcripts.

Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their stored data through the bot's official support server. Ticket transcripts are automatically deleted after 30 days and may be deleted earlier upon request.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, users can contact the bot owner through the official support server.
